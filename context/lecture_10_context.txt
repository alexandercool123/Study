CROSS-LECTURE NOTES:
- Q7b JWT structure: header.payload.signature (three base64url parts dot-separated). Header: alg, typ. Claims: registered (iss, sub, aud, exp, nbf, iat, jti), public, private. Signature via JWS (HMAC or RSA/ECDSA). RFC 7519 is authoritative — cite specific registered-claim names from it. JWS/JWE are the signing/encryption layers.
- Q7a JWT vs session ID: stateless/self-contained vs server-side session store. Connect to OAuth/OIDC tokens (lectures 7 & 11) which are JWTs.
- Q7c Verifiable Credentials: components (credential metadata, claims, proof/signature), roles issuer/holder/verifier, the trust triangle. This also appears in lecture 6 (SSI/DID) and EU wallet (lecture 8). SD-JWT (lecture 11) is the JWT-based selective-disclosure VC format.
