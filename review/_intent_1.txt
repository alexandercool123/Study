OWNS — Exam Q1: Essential Concepts for IAM
  (a) Discuss the main security objectives in IAM (confidentiality, integrity, authenticity, ...) and give examples of how they can be realized.
  (b) Why is secure key distribution a problem in symmetric key cryptography? What are the advantages and limitations of symmetric key cryptography?
  (c) Explain the concept of non-repudiation. How is it different from authenticity? Why can't symmetric encryption alone provide non-repudiation? What role do digital signatures play in IAM systems?
